Test the Google OAuth and Calendar integration

1 Connect Google Account

Click to start the OAuth flow. Your browser will redirect to Google's consent screen.

What happens:
1. Click button above
2. Redirects to: http://localhost:3000/auth/google
3. You login with Google
4. Backend stores tokens in database
5. You return to this page

2 Test Create Calendar Event

After connecting Google, test creating a calendar event through the chat tool system.

💡 You can get a Firebase token after authenticating through the backend, or use your own Firebase app.
